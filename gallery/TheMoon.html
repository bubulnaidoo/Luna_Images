The Moon

The Moon is the only moon of Earth. It is the 5th largest moon in our Solar System. Humanity has actually visited the moon in the late 60s and early 70s, making it the only place besides Earth where humans have stepped foot on. What is interesting about the Moon is that it has many craters. Infact, more than 5,000 craters bigger than 25 kilometers exist on the Moons surface.
