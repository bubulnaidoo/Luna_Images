Neptune & Its Moons

Neptune is the 8th planet from the Solar System. It is the most distant world from the Sun, around 30 times farther than Earth. Neptune is a very stormy world, with winds reaching up to 2,000 miles per hour. Here is a fun fact: Neptune was the first planet to be discovered using math. Mathematicians and astronomers saw that Uranus's orbit was a bit wrong and concluded that there was another planet tugging on it. Using some calculations, they were able to discover Neptune.
