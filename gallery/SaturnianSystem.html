Saturn & Its Moons

Saturn is the 6th planet from the Sun. It is the second largest planet in our Solar System, boasting a diameter 120,536 kilometers or 74,898 miles long. Saturn has a very large ring system 282,000 kilometers or 175,000 miles long. Saturn has the most moons of any planet, coming in around 146. Some of its moons are very special such as Titan which has a very thick atmosphere.
