Jupiter & Its Moons

Jupiter is the 5th planet from the Sun. It is the largest and most massive planet in our Solar System. Jupiter has a diameter of around 142,800 kilometers or 88,235 miles. It has around 95 moons, four of which are some of the biggest moons in our Solar System: Ganymede, Callisto, Io and Europa. Fun fact, Jupiter has a storm that is almost twice as large as Earth
